Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, we offer:

  • 7-Day Return Window: Return items within 7 days of delivery
  • Full Refund: Get your money back for unworn, unused items
  • Free Exchange: Exchange for a different size or color
Important: Items must be in original condition with tags attached.

Eligible Items

The following items can be returned or exchanged:

  • Clothing with original tags attached
  • Unworn shoes in original packaging
  • Accessories in unused condition
  • Items without damage or alterations

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns on:

  • Underwear and intimate apparel
  • Earrings and pierced jewelry
  • Sale or clearance items (marked as final sale)
  • Customized or personalized items

How to Return

Follow these simple steps to return your item:

  1. Contact us at +260 77-117-2699 or contact@montclairwardrobe.com
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Pack the item securely with original tags
  4. We'll arrange pickup or you can drop off at our boutique
  5. Refund processed within 3-5 business days

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? Exchanges are easy:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • We'll check availability of your preferred item
  • Free exchange delivery within Lusaka
  • Exchange processed within 2-3 business days
