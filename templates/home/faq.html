Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Browse our collections, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or sign in. Fill in your delivery details and choose your payment method (Mobile Money or Cash on Delivery).
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, and Cash on Delivery. All mobile money payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.
How long does delivery take?
Orders placed before 12:00 PM are delivered the same day. Orders after 12:00 PM are delivered the next business day. Weekend deliveries are available on Saturdays.
Can I return or exchange an item?
Yes! You have 7 days from delivery to return or exchange items. Items must be unworn with original tags attached. Contact us to arrange a return or exchange.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive an SMS with tracking details and the delivery person's contact information. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard.
Do you offer gift wrapping?
Yes! We offer complimentary gift wrapping for all orders. Just add a note during checkout requesting gift wrapping, and we'll take care of the rest.
What if my item is damaged or defective?
We're sorry if that happens! Contact us immediately at +260 77-117-2699 or contact@montclairwardrobe.com with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund right away.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, you can cancel your order before it's dispatched. Contact us as soon as possible. Once the order is out for delivery, cancellation may not be possible, but you can still return it within 7 days.
Do you have a physical store?
Yes! Visit our boutique at Lilayi Area 56, Lusaka. We're open Monday-Saturday 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM and Sunday 11:00 AM - 6:00 PM. Come see our collections in person!
How do I contact customer service?
You can reach us at +260 77-117-2699, email contact@montclairwardrobe.com, or use our contact form. We respond within 24 hours during business days.
